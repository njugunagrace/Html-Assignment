My experience at AkiraChix in the first week

Being part of the AkiraChix was something I never thought I would experience.

My first week was filled with fun,confusion and generally exciting.

Some of the good things I experienced in the first week were;

  1. Meeting new people and from different diversities
  2. An inviting accomodation
  3. Beautiful trainors and TAs who were ready to assist if one gets stuck
  4. Scrumptious meals
  5. Learning new things everyday
  6. Using a laptop connected to the internet was quite exciting.

Some of the not so good things I have experienced so far are;

  • Being an introvert, making friends was a bit hard
  • Sitting down for a long time was something I was not used to
  • Adjusting to a new environment and away from my family was quite hard.
